northern cuisine

ranking: 4.8/5

price: $$$

recommendations: mapo tofu, sweet eggplant, pork intestines

desc: very yummy & authentic, but pricy. good for big groups & family style! i go whenever i want to spoil myself

kyo matcha

ranking: 4.5/5

price: $$$

recommendations: matcha brulee cream slush

desc: very yummy, but pricy. i always get the same drink because it's so yummy!

shawarma joint

×

shawarma joint

ranking: 4.8/5

price: $

recommendations: chicken shawarma bowl with chickpea salad & cauliflower & jerusalem salad

desc: a bowl by itself isn't super cheap, but you can eat it for 3 meals and its super yummy. it also comes with free pita & sauce. also i heard the lunch deal is really good w/ free fries & a drink

midsummer lounge

×

midsummer lounge

ranking: 4.5/5

price: $$$

recommendations: A8 stewed beef consomme noodle

desc: on the expensive side, but it's pretty good for when you're craving a soup noodle! i (big soup noodle lover) look for chewiness of noodle & depth of soup flavor, and this dish has both!

zhangliang malatang

×

zhangliang malatang

ranking: 4.3/5

price: $

recommendations: classic spicy malatang with wide glass noodles & beef slices

desc: super yummy (soup has good depth) and you can customize to your liking because it's diy! my bowls are usually $12 and it's a lot of food. the rating is docked tho because there were maggots in the food before...

ten second rice noodle

×

ten second rice noodle

ranking: 4.2/5

price: $

recommendations: hot pot sliced beef rice noodle & sour cabbage fish rice noodle

desc: most dishes are < $15 and the soup is yummy with depth and the noodles are always springy. lots of toppings in the soup and very filling.
